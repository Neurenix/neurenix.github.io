Brand Guidelines

Last Update: 03/25/2025

Neurenix/Phynexus is a unique and valuable brand, and as such, its use should be carefully controlled to ensure that its identity is respected and not associated with activities or projects that are not aligned with the vision and mission of Neurenix. Below are the guidelines and rules for using the brand, including the name, logo, image, and any other visual or symbolic representation of Neurenix/Phynexus.

1. Use of the Name "Neurenix" and "Phynexus":

Any use of the name "Neurenix" or "Phynexus" must comply with the following guidelines:

- Authorization: The name "Neurenix" and "Phynexus" may be used as long as it aligns with the brand's vision and mission. The name should not be associated with activities that suggest endorsement, sponsorship, partnership, affiliation, or support for other projects or brands.

- Transparency: Whenever using the brand name, ensure that there is no ambiguity that could suggest a relationship that does not exist, such as unauthorized partnerships.

- Avoid Unauthorized Endorsements and Partnerships: The name "Neurenix" or "Phynexus" must not suggest endorsement, sponsorship, partnership, affiliation, or support for external projects, organizations, or initiatives without explicit authorization.

2. Use of the Image and Logo:

The image and logo of Neurenix/Phynexus are key elements of the brand’s visual identity. The use of the logo is restricted and should be handled with care to maintain the integrity of the brand.

- Do Not Modify the Logo: The Neurenix/Phynexus logo must not be altered in any way. This includes changes to colors, shapes, adding elements, or any form of manipulation.

- Respect Visual Integrity: When using the logo or brand image, ensure that the visual integrity of Neurenix/Phynexus is preserved. The logo should not be distorted, overlaid, or adjusted in any manner that would damage the brand’s perception.

3. Associations with Third Parties:

Neurenix/Phynexus should only be associated with projects, products, or organizations that share its values and mission. Any use of the name or logo of Neurenix/Phynexus alongside other brands or initiatives must be done in a manner that does not imply endorsement or support without proper authorization.

- Use of Name and Logo with Other Brands: When associating Neurenix/Phynexus with other brands or initiatives, the name and logo must be used in a way that does not imply that Neurenix/Phynexus is formally involved without explicit permission from the brand.

4. Licensing and Compliance with Apache 2.0 License:

The Neurenix/Phynexus source code is licensed under the Apache 2.0 license, and these brand guidelines complement that license but do not override the permissions granted by the source code license.

- Apache 2.0 License: The use of Neurenix/Phynexus source code under the Apache 2.0 license allows for the usage, modification, and distribution of the code, provided that the terms of the license are followed.

- Brand Restrictions: However, the use of the name and logo of Neurenix/Phynexus is not covered by the Apache 2.0 license. The Apache 2.0 license allows for the use of the code, but it does not grant permission for using the name or logo of the brand outside of the conditions outlined in these brand guidelines.

5. Changes to the Brand Guidelines:

These brand guidelines may be altered at any time. Any changes will be published in the official Neurenix repository and will be subject to the same transparency and approval process. All brand users must regularly check the guidelines to ensure compliance.

Conclusion: Collaboration and respect for the brand guidelines will ensure the sustainable growth of Neurenix/Phynexus as a brand and project.

Note: The use of the name, logo, and image of Neurenix/Phynexus is subject to these Brand Guidelines in conjunction with the Apache 2.0 license for the Neurenix source code. Compliance with these guidelines ensures that the integrity of the brand is maintained in all instances of use.
