Privacy Policy:

Last Update: 03/08/2025

At Neurenix, we are committed to ensuring your privacy and transparency regarding the data we handle. This Privacy Policy explains how we manage user information on our website and through our associated services, including our Discord bot.

1. Information We Do Not Collect:

We want to make it clear that Neurenix does not collect any personal data from users when you visit our website. We respect your privacy, and we do not track, store, or process any personal or identifiable information. The site is designed with user privacy in mind, and we do not request or gather any data that could be used to identify individuals.

2. The Neurenix Website:

The Neurenix website is purely informational, offering details about the Neurenix project and its offerings. We do not require users to create accounts or submit personal information to access the content on the website.

3. The Neurenix Bot (Discord):

While the Neurenix website does not collect any user information, our Neurenix Bot operates independently and may interact with GitHub and Discord APIs to share updates about the Neurenix repository. For more information about what data the bot processes, please refer to the dedicated Bot Privacy Policy.

This Privacy Policy outlines that the bot only shares repository change information such as commit messages and the usernames of contributors, and does not collect any personal information from users.

4. Security and Data Protection:

We take data security seriously. While we do not collect personal information, the data transferred between our website, GitHub, and Discord is protected using industry-standard encryption methods. We also encourage users to review the privacy policies of third-party services like GitHub and Discord for more details on how they handle your data.

5. Changes to This Privacy Policy:

This Privacy Policy may be updated from time to time to reflect any changes in our practices or in response to legal requirements. Please check back periodically for any updates.
