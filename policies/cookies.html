Cookie Policy

Last Update: 03/08/2025

Your privacy is important to us. Neurenix does not collect, store, or share any personal data or browsing information from visitors to this site. We do not use cookies to track or store any personal information.

What are cookies?

Cookies are small text files stored on your device when you visit a website. They are typically used to enhance user experience, such as keeping you logged in or remembering your preferences. However, on our site, we do not use cookies to collect or store any personal data or browsing information.

Transparency and Open Source

If you want to verify our policy, the full source code of this website is publicly available on the Neurenix GitHub. As we are committed to privacy and transparency, you can review the code at any time to ensure that no data is being collected on our site.

If you have any questions regarding our cookie or privacy policy, feel free to contact us!
